--Implementing Online Multiplayer
    Adding multiplayer inside a GLUT render loop was challenging:
    -Avoid blocking calls (recv() freezes rendering)
    -Required threading for listening to network updates
    -Syncing host/client turn states
    -Ensuring both boards stay identical
    -Avoiding mismatched turns
    -Handling connection refusal & timeouts

    After fixes, LAN/local online play works with:
    -Move synchronization
    -Per-turn updates
    -Host = White, Client = Black mapping
